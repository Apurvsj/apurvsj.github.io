Fact: Kidney diseases can affect individuals of any age. Conditions like polycystic kidney disease can be genetically inherited and can affect children and young adults. Lifestyle factors such as obesity, smoking, and type 2 diabetes can also lead to kidney disease in younger individuals. ## Myth 4: High protein diets are harmful to kidneys Many people believe that a high protein diet can harm the kidneys. Fact: According to the National Kidney Foundation, a high protein diet is not harmful to kidneys in a healthy individual. However, in people with kidney disease, a lower protein diet is typically recommended to prevent additional kidney damage. ## Myth 5: All kidney stones are caused by high calcium intake While it's true that excess calcium can lead to certain types of kidney stones, it's not the only cause. Fact: There are different types of kidney stones, and not all are related to calcium intake.
