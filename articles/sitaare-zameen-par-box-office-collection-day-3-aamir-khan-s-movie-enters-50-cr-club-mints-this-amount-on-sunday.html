Title: 'Sitaare Zameen Par' Joins the ₹50 Crore Club: Aamir Khan's Latest Triumph
Subheading: Aamir Khan's Movie Continues to Soar, Collecting Impressive Earnings on its Third Day at the Box Office

Introduction:
Aamir Khan's latest cinematic venture, 'Sitaare Zameen Par,' has already made its mark in the box office, entering the coveted ₹50 crore club just three days after its release. The movie, known for its mesmerizing performances and compelling storyline, has undoubtedly struck a chord with the masses, leading to its impressive earnings.

Body Content:

'Sitaare Zameen Par,' the heartwarming tale of dreams, resilience, and success, has been lapped up by audiences nationwide. Highlights of 'Sitaare Zameen Par' Box Office Collection:

- The film opened with an impressive ₹15 crore on its release day, setting the stage for a successful run.

- On Saturday, 'Sitaare Zameen Par' sustained its momentum, adding another ₹17 crore to its kitty.

- Sunday proved to be a gold mine for the film, with it raking in a whopping ₹18 crore, driving the total collection to over ₹50 crore.

The movie's success can be attributed to Aamir Khan's compelling performance and the film's gripping storyline, which has resonated with audiences. The positive word-of-mouth and glowing reviews have also played a significant role in driving the film's box office collection.
