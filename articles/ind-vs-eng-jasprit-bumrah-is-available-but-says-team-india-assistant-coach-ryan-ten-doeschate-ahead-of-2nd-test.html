Ind Vs Eng: 'Jasprit Bumrah Is Available But ...,' Says Team India Assistant Coach Ryan Ten Doeschate Ahead Of 2Nd Test

Published on June 30, 2025

With the much-anticipated 2nd Test between India and England on the horizon, Team India assistant coach Ryan ten Doeschate has shed some light on the availability of star fast bowler Jasprit Bumrah. Fans and experts alike have been eagerly awaiting news on Bumrah's fitness and readiness to take the field, and the latest update from the team is sure to generate a lot of buzz.

Key Points:

  • Jasprit Bumrah is available for selection for the 2nd Test against England.
  • Team India assistant coach Ryan ten Doeschate has hinted at some possible concerns regarding Bumrah's fitness.
  • The decision to include Bumrah in the playing XI will be made after careful consideration of various factors.

According to ten Doeschate, "Jasprit Bumrah is available for selection, but we need to assess his fitness levels and make a decision accordingly. We want to ensure that he is fully fit and ready to perform at his best before we include him in the playing XI."

It is clear that the team management is taking a cautious approach with Bumrah, considering his importance to the side and the upcoming challenges in the Test series. Bumrah's ability to generate pace and movement makes him a key asset for Team India, and his presence in the playing XI can tilt the balance in their favor.

As the team prepares for the crucial 2nd Test, all eyes will be on Bumrah's fitness and whether he will be deemed fit enough to take the field. With the series finely poised, the inclusion of Bumrah could prove to be a game-changer for Team India.
