Jane Street To Resume Trading On Nse, Bse From Tuesday

Published on July 21, 2025

After a brief pause, Jane Street, a leading global trading firm, has announced its plans to resume trading on the National Stock Exchange (NSE) and Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) starting from Tuesday. This decision comes as a relief to investors and traders who rely on Jane Street's expertise in the financial markets.

Key Points:

  • Jane Street to resume trading on NSE and BSE from Tuesday
  • Investors and traders welcome the decision
  • Increased confidence in the financial markets

With a reputation for excellence and innovation in trading strategies, Jane Street plays a crucial role in providing liquidity and market-making services. The firm's return to trading on the NSE and BSE is expected to have a positive impact on market activity and investor confidence.

During the temporary pause in trading, Jane Street remained committed to ensuring a smooth transition back to the exchanges. The firm has taken necessary precautions to mitigate any potential risks and is ready to resume its operations with the highest level of efficiency and reliability.
