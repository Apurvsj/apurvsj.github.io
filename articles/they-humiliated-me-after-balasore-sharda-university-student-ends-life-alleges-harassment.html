'They Humiliated Me...': After Balasore, Sharda University Student Ends Life, Alleges Harassment

Published on July 19, 2025

Recently, a tragic incident shocked the nation as a student from Sharda University took her own life, citing harassment as the reason. The incident has once again brought to light the issue of harassment on college campuses and the need for stricter measures to prevent such occurrences.

The Incident

The student, who was pursuing a course at Sharda University, alleged in her suicide note that she was subjected to constant humiliation and harassment by her peers. She mentioned a particular incident in Balasore where she was publicly humiliated, which pushed her to take the extreme step of ending her life.

  • The student's suicide note has sparked outrage and demands for justice from various quarters.
  • Authorities at Sharda University have promised to investigate the matter thoroughly and take necessary action against those responsible.

Impact on the Community

The tragic incident has left the student community at Sharda University in shock and disbelief. Many students have come forward to express their grief and concern over the incident. There have been calls for stricter anti-harassment policies and counseling services to be made available to students.

It is essential for educational institutions to create a safe and inclusive environment for all students, free from any form of harassment or discrimination. The incident at Sharda University serves as a grim reminder of the consequences of negligence in addressing such issues.

FAQ

What steps is Sharda University taking to address the issue of harassment on campus?

Sharda University has initiated an internal investigation into the allegations of harassment made by the student. The university has also promised to implement stricter anti-harassment policies and provide counseling services to students in need.

How can students seek help if they experience harassment at Sharda University?

Students who experience harassment at Sharda University are encouraged to report the incidents to the university authorities. There are designated channels through which students can seek help and support, including counseling services and grievance redressal mechanisms.

What can be done to prevent similar incidents in the future?

Preventing similar incidents in the future requires a collective effort from the university administration, faculty, students, and support staff. It is essential to create awareness about the consequences of harassment and the need to respect one another. Regular training sessions, workshops, and awareness campaigns can help in creating a more inclusive and safe campus environment.
