Shubman Gill burst onto the international cricket scene with a bang, displaying maturity beyond his years and a technique that drew comparisons to the greats of the game.   • His fearless approach to batting and elegant strokeplay have won him many admirers, both within the cricketing fraternity and among fans.

Gill's Record-breaking Feat

In the recent series against England, Shubman Gill etched his name in the history books by breaking records set by two cricketing legends, Virat Kohli and Sachin Tendulkar. Gill's consistency and ability to score big runs at crucial junctures of the game have set him apart as a special talent.

  • Gill became the youngest Indian batsman to score a half-century in both innings of a Test match, surpassing the previous record held by Virat Kohli.
  • He also became the fastest Indian batsman to reach 1000 Test runs in terms of innings, a record previously held by Sachin Tendulkar.
