Jagdeep Dhankhar Resigns As Vice President, Cites Medical Reasons

Published on July 21, 2025

In a surprising turn of events, Vice President Jagdeep Dhankhar has announced his resignation from office citing medical reasons. Dhankhar, who has been serving as the Vice President for the past three years, made the announcement in a press conference earlier today.

Reasons for Resignation

  • Dhankhar explained that his decision to resign was a difficult one but was necessary due to his deteriorating health.
  • He mentioned that he had been battling a chronic illness for the past few months and felt that he could no longer fulfill his duties effectively.
  • Despite receiving medical treatment, Dhankhar admitted that the demands of the Vice President's role were taking a toll on his health and well-being.

During his tenure as Vice President, Dhankhar was known for his dedication to his duties and his commitment to serving the people of the country. His resignation has come as a shock to many, who admired his leadership and vision for the future.

Impact of Resignation

Dhankhar's resignation has raised concerns about the future of the Vice President's office and who will succeed him. The President is expected to appoint a new Vice President in the coming weeks to fill the vacancy left by Dhankhar's resignation.

Many are speculating about the potential candidates for the position and what qualities they should possess to effectively carry out the responsibilities of the Vice President. It remains to be seen how Dhankhar's resignation will impact the political landscape and the functioning of the government.
