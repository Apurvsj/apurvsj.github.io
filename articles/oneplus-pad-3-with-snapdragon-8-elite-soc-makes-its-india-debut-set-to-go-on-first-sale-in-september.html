Oneplus Pad 3 With Snapdragon 8 Elite Soc Makes Its India Debut, Set To Go On First Sale In September

Published on July 19, 2025

Exciting news for tech enthusiasts in India as OnePlus has announced the launch of its latest tablet, the OnePlus Pad 3, powered by the high-performing Snapdragon 8 Elite SoC. This much-anticipated device is set to make its debut in the Indian market and will go on its first sale in September.

Key Features of OnePlus Pad 3:

  • Powered by Snapdragon 8 Elite SoC
  • Large display for immersive viewing experience
  • Sleek and stylish design
  • Long-lasting battery life
  • Advanced camera capabilities

The OnePlus Pad 3 is designed to offer users a seamless and powerful experience, with the Snapdragon 8 Elite SoC ensuring smooth performance and multitasking capabilities. The large display of the tablet provides an immersive viewing experience, making it ideal for watching movies, playing games, or browsing the web.

In addition to its performance capabilities, the OnePlus Pad 3 features a sleek and stylish design that is sure to turn heads. The tablet also boasts advanced camera capabilities, allowing users to capture stunning photos and videos with ease.

One of the standout features of the OnePlus Pad 3 is its long-lasting battery life, ensuring that users can stay connected and productive throughout the day without having to worry about running out of power.

Availability and Pricing:

The OnePlus Pad 3 is set to go on its first sale in India in September, giving consumers the opportunity to get their hands on this cutting-edge device. The pricing details for the OnePlus Pad 3 are yet to be announced, but it is expected to be competitively priced in the premium tablet segment.
