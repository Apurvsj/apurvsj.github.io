FAQ

What is the difference between a comet and an asteroid?

Comets are icy bodies that develop a glowing coma and tail when they approach the Sun, while asteroids are rocky or metallic bodies that do not exhibit these characteristics.

Could this object pose a threat to Earth?

At this time, there is no evidence to suggest that the object poses any danger to Earth. Scientists are closely monitoring its trajectory to ensure the safety of our planet.

It is difficult to predict how long it will take to definitively classify the object. Additional observations and analysis will be necessary to reach a consensus within the scientific community.
