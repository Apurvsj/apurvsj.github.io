Pm Modi In Maldives Live: India Will Always Support Maldives To Strengthen Its Defence Capabilities, Says Modi

Published on July 25, 2025

Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi recently visited the Maldives, where he reiterated India's commitment to supporting the Maldives in strengthening its defence capabilities. During his visit, PM Modi emphasized the importance of the strong bilateral relationship between India and the Maldives and the need for both countries to work together for regional security and stability.

Key Points from PM Modi's Speech in Maldives:

  • India will always support the Maldives in enhancing its defence capabilities.
  • Both countries will continue to cooperate on various issues, including maritime security and counter-terrorism.
  • PM Modi highlighted the importance of the Maldives as a key partner in India's 'Neighbourhood First' policy.

PM Modi's visit to the Maldives comes at a time when the geopolitical dynamics in the Indian Ocean region are evolving rapidly. India's support for the Maldives in strengthening its defence capabilities is seen as a strategic move to counter growing Chinese influence in the region.

Through initiatives such as the Coastal Surveillance Radar System in the Maldives, India has been actively involved in enhancing the Maldives' maritime security capabilities. The two countries have also been cooperating on joint military exercises and training programs to improve the Maldives' defence preparedness.

PM Modi's assurance of continued support for the Maldives is a clear indication of India's commitment to maintaining peace and stability in the Indian Ocean region. By strengthening the Maldives' defence capabilities, India aims to enhance the security of the region and ensure a free and open Indo-Pacific.
