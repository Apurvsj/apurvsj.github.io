Title: "Rishabh Pant Follows in Dhoni's Footsteps: Joins Elite List of Indian Test Cricketers"

Subheading: "Young Wicketkeeper-Batsman Pant Emerges as Third Indian to Achieve Notable Test Milestone, Joins Dhoni's Elite Club"

Introduction:
In the world of cricket, a new name has etched itself into the annals of Indian Test cricket. Rishabh Pant, the young wicketkeeper-batsman, has joined the ranks of cricketing royalty, becoming the third Indian to achieve a remarkable feat in Tests. Previously, only two other Indian cricketers, including the legendary MS Dhoni, had reached this milestone.

Body:

Following an exceptional performance in the recent IND vs ENG Test series, Pant has carved a distinct niche for himself in the arena of international cricket. His consistent, high-calibre performances have catapulted him into a select group of Indian cricketers. This achievement has brought him shoulder-to-shoulder with the iconic MS Dhoni, a testament to his burgeoning prowess.

The notable Test milestones achieved by Pant are:

- He now holds the record for the third-highest score by an Indian wicketkeeper in Tests.
- Pant is the third Indian wicketkeeper to score over 1000 runs in Test cricket.
- He became the fastest Indian wicketkeeper to reach the 1000-run mark in Test cricket, achieving the feat in 27 innings.

The only other Indian cricketers to have achieved these feats are former captains and wicketkeepers MS Dhoni and Syed Kirmani. Pant's achievement is made more significant when one considers the illustrious company he now keeps.
