Another Air India Flight Scare: Delhi-Kolkata Flight Aborted Moments Before Take-Off Due To 'Technical Issue'

Published on July 21, 2025

Recently, passengers aboard an Air India flight from Delhi to Kolkata experienced a moment of panic as the flight was aborted just moments before take-off due to a reported 'technical issue'. This incident has once again raised concerns about the safety and reliability of air travel, particularly with Air India.

Let's take a closer look at what happened and what passengers can expect in such situations.

What Happened?

  • The Air India flight from Delhi to Kolkata was preparing for take-off when the pilot detected a technical issue with the aircraft.
  • Passengers were asked to deplane immediately for their safety.
  • Air India officials assured passengers that the issue would be resolved as soon as possible.

Passenger Reactions

Passengers expressed their concerns and frustrations over the incident, with many questioning the safety protocols in place for Air India flights. Some passengers shared their experiences on social media, highlighting the need for better communication and transparency in such situations.

What to Expect in Such Situations

When faced with a flight delay or cancellation due to technical issues, passengers should expect the following:

  • Clear communication from airline officials regarding the issue and estimated resolution time.
  • Assistance with rebooking or refund options for affected passengers.
  • Prioritization of passenger safety above all else.

FAQ

Q: Will I be compensated for the inconvenience caused by the flight delay?

A: Airlines typically offer compensation in the form of vouchers, refunds, or rebooking options for passengers affected by flight delays or cancellations due to technical issues. It is recommended to check with the airline for specific compensation policies.

Q: How can I stay updated on the status of my flight in such situations?

A: Passengers can stay informed by checking the airline's website, contacting customer service, or following the airline on social media for real-time updates on flight delays or cancellations.

Overall, incidents like the aborted Air India flight from Delhi to Kolkata serve as reminders of the importance of safety protocols in air travel. Passengers are encouraged to stay informed, ask questions, and prioritize their safety when faced with unexpected situations during their journey.
