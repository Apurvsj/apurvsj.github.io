Golden Temple Receives Second Bomb Threat; Security Heightened

Published on July 15, 2025

The Golden Temple, one of the most revered Sikh shrines in India, has been put on high alert after receiving a second bomb threat in recent weeks. Security measures have been heightened in and around the temple complex to ensure the safety of devotees and visitors.

Increased Security Measures

  • Additional police personnel have been deployed to patrol the area and conduct thorough security checks.
  • Baggage screening and metal detectors are being used at all entry points to the temple.
  • CCTV cameras have been installed to monitor the movements of individuals around the complex.
  • Regular security drills are being conducted to prepare for any potential threats.

The authorities are taking the threat seriously and are leaving no stone unturned to prevent any untoward incidents from occurring. The safety and security of all those visiting the Golden Temple are of paramount importance.

Previous Threats

This is not the first time that the Golden Temple has been targeted with a bomb threat. In 2016, the shrine received a similar threat which turned out to be a hoax. However, given the current security situation in the country, the authorities are not taking any chances and are ensuring that all necessary precautions are in place.

FAQ

Q: Why is the Golden Temple receiving bomb threats?

A: The reasons behind the bomb threats are unclear, but it is believed to be an attempt to create fear and panic among the devotees and visitors to the temple.

Q: Is it safe to visit the Golden Temple despite the threats?

A: The authorities are working tirelessly to ensure the safety of all those visiting the Golden Temple. It is advised to follow all security protocols and cooperate with the security personnel to have a safe and peaceful visit.

Q: What can I do to help enhance security at the Golden Temple?

A: You can help by being vigilant and reporting any suspicious activities or items to the security personnel immediately. Cooperation from everyone is essential in maintaining the security of the shrine.
