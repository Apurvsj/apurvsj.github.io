Realme 15 Pro 5G, Realme 15 5G Launched In India With 7,000Mah Battery: Price, Specs And More

Published on July 24, 2025

Realme has recently launched two new smartphones in India - the Realme 15 Pro 5G and the Realme 15 5G. Both phones come with a massive 7,000mAh battery, making them ideal for users who need a device that can last all day without needing frequent charging. Let's take a closer look at the features, specifications, and pricing of these new smartphones.

Realme 15 Pro 5G

  • 6.6-inch Super AMOLED display
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon 778G processor
  • 8GB of RAM and 128GB of internal storage
  • Quad-camera setup with a 64MP primary sensor
  • 7,000mAh battery with 65W fast charging support
  • Price: Starting at Rs. 24,999

Realme 15 5G

  • 6.5-inch LCD display
  • MediaTek Dimensity 800U processor
  • 6GB of RAM and 64GB of internal storage
  • Triple-camera setup with a 48MP primary sensor
  • 7,000mAh battery with 18W fast charging support
  • Price: Starting at Rs. 19,999

Both the Realme 15 Pro 5G and Realme 15 5G come with 5G connectivity, allowing users to experience faster download and upload speeds on compatible networks. The phones run on Realme UI 3.0 based on Android 12, providing a smooth and intuitive user experience.

With a focus on performance, battery life, and camera capabilities, the Realme 15 Pro 5G and Realme 15 5G aim to cater to the needs of modern smartphone users who demand more from their devices.

FAQ

Q: Does the Realme 15 Pro 5G support wireless charging?

A: No, the Realme 15 Pro 5G does not support wireless charging. However, it comes with 65W fast charging support for quick charging.

Q: Are the Realme 15 Pro 5G and Realme 15 5G waterproof?

A: Both phones are not officially rated for waterproofing, so it is recommended to avoid exposing them to water or moisture to prevent damage.
