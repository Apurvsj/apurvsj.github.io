'Walking Corpses': Over 100 Dead Of Starvation, Says Unrwa; India Backs Aid Push In Gaza

Published on July 24, 2025

Recent reports from the United Nations Relief and Works Agency (UNRWA) have highlighted a dire situation in Gaza, with over 100 individuals reportedly dead from starvation. The crisis has sparked a global response, with India stepping up to support aid efforts in the region.

As the world watches in horror at the unfolding tragedy in Gaza, it is crucial to understand the root causes of this humanitarian crisis and the urgent need for international support.

The Crisis in Gaza

  • According to UNRWA, over 100 individuals have died of starvation in Gaza in recent weeks.
  • The ongoing conflict in the region has severely impacted access to food and basic necessities for the Palestinian population.
  • Years of political unrest and economic instability have exacerbated the situation, leaving many families struggling to survive.

The lack of access to adequate food and healthcare has pushed many individuals to the brink of starvation, leading to a heartbreaking loss of life in Gaza.

India's Support for Aid Efforts

India has pledged its support for aid efforts in Gaza, recognizing the urgent need for humanitarian assistance in the region. The Indian government has committed to providing financial aid and resources to help alleviate the suffering of the Palestinian people.

By backing the international community's push for aid in Gaza, India is taking a stand against the injustice and suffering faced by the Palestinian population. This support reflects India's commitment to promoting peace and stability in the region.

FAQ

What are the main causes of the humanitarian crisis in Gaza?

The humanitarian crisis in Gaza is fueled by a combination of political unrest, economic instability, and the ongoing conflict in the region. These factors have severely limited access to food, healthcare, and basic necessities for the Palestinian population.

How can individuals and organizations contribute to aid efforts in Gaza?

Individuals and organizations can support aid efforts in Gaza by donating to reputable humanitarian organizations, volunteering their time and skills, and raising awareness about the crisis. Every contribution, no matter how small, can make a difference in the lives of those affected by the crisis.
