Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi 3: Smriti Irani'S Pay Jumps From Rs 1800 To Rs 14 Lakh Per Episode; Reports

Published on July 09, 2025

One of the most iconic TV shows in Indian television history, Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi, is all set to make a comeback with its third season. The show, which originally aired in the early 2000s, was a massive hit and catapulted its lead actress, Smriti Irani, to fame. Now, reports suggest that Irani's pay for the new season has seen a staggering increase from Rs 1800 per episode to Rs 14 lakh per episode.

Smriti Irani's Pay Hike

According to industry insiders, Smriti Irani's pay for Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi 3 is set to make her one of the highest-paid actresses on Indian television. Her reported jump from Rs 1800 to Rs 14 lakh per episode is a testament to the show's enduring popularity and Irani's own star power.

The news of Irani's pay hike has generated a lot of buzz among fans and industry observers alike. Many are excited to see the actress reprise her iconic role of Tulsi Virani and are eagerly awaiting the premiere of the new season.
